Discover the Game

Game Introduction

Tatodagi is a competitive strategy board game for 2-4 players, designed to test your decision-making skills and tactical foresight. Set in a mystical world, players take on the roles of strategists vying for control over the ancient lands of Tatodagi.

The game combines elements of resource management, area control, and strategic combat, offering a unique experience with every playthrough. With multiple paths to victory and dynamic gameplay, Tatodagi rewards both careful planning and adaptive tactics.

Average gameplay lasts 60-90 minutes, making it perfect for game nights, family gatherings, or competitive tournaments. Suitable for ages 12 and up.

Game Board

Player Pieces

4 sets of uniquely colored pieces including commanders, soldiers, and special units.

Custom Dice

Action Cards

How to Play

1

Setup

Place the game board in the center of the table. Each player selects a color and takes the corresponding pieces. Shuffle the action cards and place them face down. Distribute starting resources: 3 energy tokens, 2 mineral tokens, and 1 knowledge token to each player. Place the remaining tokens in the general supply.

2

Gameplay Rounds

Each round consists of three phases: Planning, Action, and Resolution. During Planning, players secretly select their action cards. In the Action phase, players take turns placing pieces and executing actions. The Resolution phase determines control of territories and awards victory points.

3

Movement & Combat

Pieces move according to their type: Commanders move 1 hex, Soldiers move 2 hexes. When moving into an occupied hex, combat occurs. Players roll dice equal to their unit count, with higher symbols winning. Defeated units return to their owner's supply.

4

Winning the Game

The game ends when a player reaches 15 victory points or controls 3 sacred sites. Victory points are earned through territory control, completing objectives, and defeating opponent commanders. The player with the most victory points at game end is declared the winner.
